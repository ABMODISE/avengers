Meet Your Heroes

Thor

Thor, the God of Thunder, Son Of Odin ,The heir of Asgard wields his enchanted hammer Mjölnir and commands lightning. He balances divine power with loyalty and a deep sense of justice, protecting both Asgard and Earth from cosmic threats

Iron Man

Tony Stark, a genius billionaire and inventor, built the Iron Man suit to escape captivity and reinvented himself as a tech-powered superhero. Armed with intellect, charisma, and arc reactor technology, he defends Earth with unmatched innovation.

Hulk

Dr. Bruce Banner transforms into the Hulk when angered — a green, gamma-radiated powerhouse. While he struggles to control his monstrous side, Hulk brings incredible strength and durability to the Avengers' battles.

Hawkeye

Clint Barton, known as Hawkeye, is a master archer and marksman with perfect aim and a sharp wit. Though lacking superpowers, his skills, courage, and tactical instincts make him a vital member of the Avengers.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, a World War II hero enhanced by the Super Soldier Serum, stands for justice and freedom as Captain America. With his indestructible shield and unwavering morals, he leads the Avengers with honor and bravery.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff, aka Black Widow, is a highly trained spy, martial artist, and former KGB assassin. With unmatched agility and stealth, she uses her skills and sharp intellect to take down enemies and fight for redemption.

About This Project

This Avengers-themed web project was created as part of the FNB App Academy, showcasing HTML, CSS, and JavaScript skills. It features an interactive and responsive design, bringing Earth's Mightiest Heroes to life with modern web technologies. The goal is to highlight clean UI design, responsive layout techniques, and creative use of web assets in a way that reflects the heroic energy of the Marvel Universe.
